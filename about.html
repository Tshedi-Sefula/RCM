About Us

Welcome to RCM Toitskraal! We are a community dedicated to worship, fellowship, and serving others through the love of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To impact lives and build a Christ-centered community where everyone feels welcome and empowered to grow in faith.

Our Mission

We strive to provide a nurturing environment for spiritual growth, meaningful relationships, and community outreach.

Leadership

Pastor VVM Mashele, along with a dedicated team of elders and ministry leaders, guides our church family.

History

Founded in 2024, RCM Toitskraal has been committed to serving the community of Toitskraal, and Siyabuswa as a whole, through worship, teaching, and outreach programs.
